Frequently Asked Questions

What is your response time for inquiries?

We respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business hours. For urgent matters, please call our emergency support line which is available 24/7.

Do you offer travel insurance?

Yes, we offer comprehensive travel insurance through our trusted partners. We can help you select the best coverage for your specific journey during the booking process.

How far in advance should I book my trip?

We recommend booking at least 3-6 months in advance for international trips, especially during peak seasons. For last-minute bookings, please contact us directly as we may have limited availability.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions on tours?

Absolutely. We work with our local partners to accommodate all dietary restrictions and preferences. Please inform us of any requirements during the booking process.

What is your cancellation policy?

Our cancellation policy varies by tour package. Generally, we offer full refunds for cancellations made 60+ days before departure, with decreasing percentages closer to the departure date. Specific details are provided during booking.
