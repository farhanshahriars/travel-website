Our Story

Creating unforgettable travel experiences since 2010

From Passion to Profession

Epic Escapes began as a simple dream between three friends who shared an insatiable wanderlust. What started as travel blog documenting our adventures across Asia has blossomed into a premier travel consultancy serving thousands of travelers worldwide.

For over a decade, we've been turning travel dreams into reality, combining local expertise with global standards to create journeys that go beyond typical tourism.
